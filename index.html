Fight Prediction

I am currently a postdoc at University College London (Ear Institute) using DL to improve the efficacy of hearing aids. Though, much of my previous and current work has focused on the use of DL in biomedical optical imaging/spectroscopy.

Previously (2022) I was a postdoc at the University of Exeter (Department of Physics) where I used deep learning to facilitate the analysis of biomedical Infrared/THz hyperspectral images.

From 2018-2022, I was a PhD student at University College London (Department of Medical Physics) where I investigated learned approaches to solving the inverse problem of quantifying haemoglobin concentrations from reconstructed photoacoustic image data (quantitative photoacoustic tomography).

Concurrently (Sep-Dec 2019), I undertook an industrial placement as a consultant data scientist at MediWise where I constructed ML models to facilitate non-invasive measurements of blood glucose concentrations.

I also dabble in algorithmic sports betting and have deployed an AI-driven web application for UFC fight prediction (developed the full stack). Aside from this, I occasionally compete in competitive e-sports events (Super Smash Bros. Ultimate).
