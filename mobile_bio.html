I am currently a research data scientist/medical physicist working on increasing the reliabilty and interpretability of machine learning models used in digital healthcare (via uncertainty quantification and other means...).

Prior to this, I spent a year (2023) as a postdoc at University College London (Ear Institute) using DL to improve the efficacy of hearing aids.

From 2018-2022, I was a PhD student at University College London (Department of Medical Physics) where I investigated learned approaches to solving the inverse problem of quantifying haemoglobin concentrations from reconstructed photoacoustic image data (quantitative photoacoustic tomography).

Concurrently (Sep-Dec 2019), I undertook an industrial placement as a consultant data scientist at MediWise where I constructed ML models to facilitate non-invasive measurements of blood glucose concentrations.

I also dabble in algorithmic sports betting and have deployed an AI-driven web application for UFC fight prediction (developed the full stack). Aside from this, I compete in competitive e-sports events (Super Smash Bros. Ultimate).
